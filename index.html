About BALBI Token

CO₂ Compensation in HVAC

BALBI token emerges as an innovative solution to address one of the biggest challenges in the Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC) field: compensating for CO₂ emissions.

Our mission is to facilitate and raise awareness among HVAC professionals about environmental responsibility, transforming the sector through blockchain technology.

By integrating BALBI into their operations, professionals can transparently neutralize their carbon footprint, supporting sustainability projects.

The Creator

Vinícius Balbi

With over twenty years of experience in the oil and gas market, working in both onshore and offshore operations, Vinícius Balbi is a highly qualified and recognized HVAC professional.

His expertise covers various facets of the sector, being a dedicated member of ASHRAE Brazil and a renowned specialist in technical room pressurization.

His passion for innovation and commitment to sustainable practices are the pillars that drive the BALBI project.
