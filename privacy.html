Privacy Policy of Balbi Token

Last updated: August 28, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how Balbi Token collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with the Service:

  • Wallet Address: Collected to send tokens and to enforce the 4-hour cooldown period between claims.
  • Last Claim Timestamp: Stored to track the cooldown period and prevent abuse of the Service.

2. Use of Cookies and Local Storage

The site uses a cookie (via browser local storage) to verify your consent to this privacy policy. The token is a simple marker that indicates you have accepted the policy.

Your private key is not stored on our servers. It is only used in your browser to sign transactions and is not transmitted to our backend infrastructure.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data (wallet address and timestamp) is used exclusively for the operation of the token, ensuring the Service is fair and distributed equitably among users, preventing spam and abuse.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (LGPD)

According to the General Data Protection Law (LGPD), you have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of incomplete or outdated data.
  • Request the anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data or data processed in non-compliance with the LGPD.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided on our website.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We recommend you review it regularly.
